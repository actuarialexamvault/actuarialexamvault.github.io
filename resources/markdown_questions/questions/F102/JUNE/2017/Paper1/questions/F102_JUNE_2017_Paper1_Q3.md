## QUESTION 3

Insurer A, a large life insurer in a country with a well-developed investment market is considering taking over a portfolio of in-force without-profit immediate annuities from Insurer B. The liabilities relating to this portfolio of annuity business will be transferred to Insurer A together with a transfer of assets from Insurer B.
Describe how Insurer A could determine the value of the transfer of assets required, using a marketconsistent approach, in exchange for the transfer of the annuity portfolio liabilities.

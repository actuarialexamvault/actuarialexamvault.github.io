## QUESTION 1

A newly qualified actuary is conducting an analysis of surplus exercise for a book of endowment assurances which has been closed to new business for a number of years. The actuary notices that the actual per policy expenses are much higher at the current valuation date than at the previous valuation date.
Describe the investigations that the actuary would carry out in response to this finding, and the actions which might be taken following these investigations.
